(86e8e5) Let $n \geq 6$ be a positive integer. We call a positive integer $n$-Norwegian if it has three distinct positive divisors whose sum is equal to $n$. Let $f(n)$ denote the smallest $n$-Norwegian positive integer. Let $M=3^{2025!}$ and for a non-negative integer $c$ define 
\begin{equation*}
    g(c)=\frac{1}{2025!}\left\lfloor \frac{2025! f(M+c)}{M}\right\rfloor.
\end{equation*}
We can write 
\begin{equation*}
    g(0)+g(4M)+g(1848374)+g(10162574)+g(265710644)+g(44636594)=\frac{p}{q}
\end{equation*}
where $p$ and $q$ are coprime positive integers. What is the remainder when $p+q$ is divided by $99991$?
